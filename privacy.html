Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026 • DramaXHub (DMXHUB.COM)

1. Introduction

Welcome to DramaXHub, branded as DMXHUB. We are committed to protecting your privacy and providing a safe, enjoyable streaming experience for Nollywood lovers worldwide. This policy explains how we handle your information.

2. Information We Collect

DramaXHub is designed to be lightweight and privacy-friendly:

  • No account required to browse and watch movies
  • Search preferences are stored locally in your browser only
  • Contact form data only when you voluntarily reach out to us
  • Anonymous usage statistics to improve our service and recommendations

3. How We Use Video Content

DramaXHub does not host any video files on our servers. All movies and trailers are officially embedded from licensed YouTube channels using YouTube's privacy-enhanced embed player (youtube-nocookie.com). When you play a video, YouTube's privacy policy applies.

We respect content creators and copyright holders. If you are a rights holder and have concerns about any content, please contact us at legal@dramaxhub.com.

4. Cookies and Tracking

We use minimal cookies to ensure the site functions properly:

  • Essential cookies for site navigation and search functionality
  • YouTube may set cookies when you play embedded videos to provide playback services
  • We do not use third-party advertising trackers or sell your data

5. Data Security

Your privacy is important to us. Any information you provide through our contact form is securely stored and never shared with third parties for marketing purposes. We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to request information about data we may hold, request deletion of your contact inquiries, and opt out of any future communications. Contact us at privacy@dramaxhub.com for any privacy-related requests.

7. Children's Privacy

DramaXHub is intended for general audiences. While we offer Family-friendly content, we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time to reflect improvements to our service. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
