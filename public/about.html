About DMXHUB

MovieBox.ph inspired but better • 170 IbakaTV Movies • Premium dark UI

DMXHUB is a free streaming hub built for Nollywood lovers. We embed premium movies from @Ibakatv1 on YouTube with official trailers, optimized for MovieBox-style experience.

Trailer autoplays muted when you click a poster, with a large mute/unmute button visible even on small screens. Full movie never autoplays — you click Play to start, giving you control.

Why DMXHUB?

• MovieBox Style — Clean header, sticky filter chips, horizontal rows, HD cards with hover play
• Trailer muted autoplay + unmute — Poster click plays trailer muted, tap 🔊 to hear
• Full movie click-to-play — Never autoplays, big play button required
• Coming Soon handling — If only trailer exists, Full Movie shows popup
• All buttons fixed — Scroll, Load More, filters, search, tabs all working

Our Mission

Make premium Nollywood accessible with a premium UI. No sign-up wall, no heavy hosting. Search, click poster → trailer muted, unmute if you like, then click to play full movie.
